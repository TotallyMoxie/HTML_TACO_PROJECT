When did tacos first appear in the United States?

The first noted mention, that Jeffrey M. Pilcher, professor of history at the University of Minnesota, could find for his research into tacos, was in 1905 in a newspaper. This information comes from an article in the Smithsonian Magazine title "Where Did the Taco Come From?" by Katy June Friesen.
